    • sounds of the internet protocol

    2023 - ongoing · web, online

    “sounds of the internet protocol” is a web-based generative musical piece. translating the four bytes of IP numbers into midi notes, the piece seeks to draw attention to the troubled histories of hegemonic power, global injustices, and technological deprivation behind these seemingly random, abstract numbers.

    how do we listen and attune to the rumblings and rustlings of our digital spaces and temporalities?
