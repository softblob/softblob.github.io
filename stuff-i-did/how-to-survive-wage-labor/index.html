    • how to survive wage labor

    2022 · zine, prints

    "how to survive wage labor" is a zine and a practical guide for corporate workers to practice tactical parasitics, resources hacking, and building undercommons within, above, below and beyond insititutions.

    many of the zines have been printed for free from office printers, as a rehersal to reclaim time, creativity and agency within systematic wage-labor exploitation and deprivation.

    shown at the 2022 bangkok art book fair.
